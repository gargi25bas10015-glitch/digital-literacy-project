Part B– Prevention Checklist:

Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

🔒 General Safety Practices:

Always use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) wherever possible
3. Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown emails/messages
4. Do not share personal information on untrusted websites or platforms
5. Regularly update apps and devices to maintain security

 💳 UPI & Financial Safety:

Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank
2.  Do not approve unknown “Collect Requests” on payment apps
3. Remember: Scanning a QR code or entering a PIN means you are **sending** money, not receiving it
4. Verify the receiver’s details before making any payment

 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime:

 Report any cyber fraud immediately at: **https://www.cybercrime.gov.in**
2. Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: **1930 (24x7)**
3. Inform your bank as soon as possible in case of financial fraud


Conclusion:
Following these precautions can help individuals stay safe online, avoid cyber threats, and protect their personal and financial information effectively.
